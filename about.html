Who We Are

Studio Ghibli is a world-renowned Japanese animation studio known for its stunning animation, heartwarming stories, and unforgettable characters. Founded in 1985 by directors Hayao Miyazaki and Isao Takahata, the studio has produced some of the most beloved animated films of all time.

Ghibli Vision

Our goal is to create meaningful and visually breathtaking films that inspire audiences of all ages. Through hand-drawn animation and compelling narratives, Studio Ghibli continues to push the boundaries of storytelling.

About Hayao Miyazaki

Hayao Miyazaki is an influential Japanese anime director. He is a founder of Studio Ghibli and has attained international acclaim as a masterful storyteller and creator of Japanese animated feature films. His notable films include Princess Mononoke, Spirited Away, Howl’s Moving Castle, and The Boy and the Heron. Miyazaki is widely regarded as one of the most accomplished filmmakers in the history of animation. He is considered the Walt Disney of Japan
